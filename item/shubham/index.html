SHUBHAM

SHUBHAM AUTOMATION & SERVICES (SAS) has been started in April 1997 with enthusiastic, dynamic and innovative team to serve world class solution in the field of industrial Automation. The company has designed and implemented many projects in various Industries like Pharmaceuticals, Packaging, Textile, Automoblies and many more.

These Projects have been developed with aid of latest automation products like, Sensors, Motion device, Inspection systems, PLC and SCADA Software. Since 2005 company has been designing & engineering packing solutions to match world’s pharma machinery standards.

Company’s focus field of area is mainly Pharmaceuticals & packaging industries. We are always thankful to our OEM customers & End Users who put trust on our knowledge & inspire us to design & develop real trend solutions which fits with current requirements of Industry & Internationa Standards like GMP Laws and Regulatory Authorities like FDA.

Shubham Automation is committed for continuous up gradation in knowledge, service & efforts in our field.
